Bio

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Maryland, working under the supervision of Prof. Hajiaghayi. I finished my undergraduate studies at Sharif University of Technology.
I'm interested in Combinatorial Optimization, Algorithmic Game Theory, and Algorithmic Graph Theory. My research has been supported by Facebook PhD Fellowship. You can find my resume here for more information.
